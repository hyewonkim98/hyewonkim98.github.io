Concept Design – 2020
CURoomies is a simple, collaborative, and mindful hunting app for Cornell students.
Role
UX/UI Designer
UX/UI Design, Visual Design & Prototyping

Problem

With very limited and scattered resources for housing in Ithaca, it's difficult for Cornell students to find and compare different options that are available. This makes it even more challenging for students to find housing options that will satisfy multiple people's preferences, which is often the case. How might we streamline the housing hunting process for Cornell students, while fostering collaboration?


Solution

A Cornell exclusive house hunting app

‍A simple onboard flow to communicate the value proposition to the users. To ensure safe communications between Cornell students using the application, verified Cornell email is required for all members.

Cater towards you & you only

When users create an account, they are asked to fill out their preferences for housing location, budget, features, and amenities. This is so that the housing options suggested by the app best match the user's need. Determining which factor they prioritize more over another allows the app to provide an even more refined list of options.

We all love a good 'for you' page

Once students have logged in or made an account, they can explore housing options that best match all their preferences.

Collaborate with your to-be roommates

Students can create groups with their future roommates to plan, communicate, and find housing together.

You can be mindful, without even trying

Once students create a group together, the app will provide a list of housing options that is uniquely catered to the group as a whole. While maintaining anonymity of individual group members, students can better understand the needs and wants of the group.

Found a place you like? Full send it.

Students can easily share their saved or recently viewed listings on group chats. No more screenshots or cmd+c-cmd+v ing urls.
