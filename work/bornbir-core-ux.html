What's Bornbir?

Bornbir is a marketplace product that connects parents with the best local health providers throughout pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum. Bornbir strives to empower expectant and new parents to make informed choices throughout one of the most significant journeys of their lives.

What did I do?

My main task was to redesign both the experience and look of the practitioner page, where the booking experience lives.

The booking experience is core to Bornbir as the main conversion moment for parent users.

The goal of this redesign project was to increase user conversion, specifically the number of people who click the CTA button for booking and the percentage of who become customers after the CTA.

The new design

I came up with a new user flow that positioned Bornbirâ€™s value proposition before the conversion moment. Rather than creating a barrier to entry for new parent users, the new flow allows them to use the feature without having to create an account.
